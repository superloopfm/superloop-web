## Context
We are finalizing the landing page for "Superloop," a platform focused on music Remix Week Events, Soundpacks (visualized as spinning records), and limited-edition drops (both digital tools for Ableton and physical vinyl).

The user's goal is to have a landing page that highlights our key features to begin marketing and advertising. 

**Core philosophies:**
- Present the idea that there are sound packs to collect.
- Introduce the "Remix Week" concept.
- Allow people to sign up for Remix Week and get one of the limited edition records from the "vending machine," along with some free downloads (which can also be limited edition).
- High focus on **limited edition competitions, luck, randomness, and FOMO**. We want to create urgency/desire to get something rare, while simultaneously providing immense value and free stuff.
